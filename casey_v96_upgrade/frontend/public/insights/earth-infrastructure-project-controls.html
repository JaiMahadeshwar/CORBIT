Where AI project controls lands first

The best early markets are complex, high-value programmes where early decisions have major cost and schedule impact: data centres, semiconductor fabs, energy projects, transport programmes, defence infrastructure and life sciences campuses.

The value proposition

A project owner can provide a brief and receive a structured intelligence pack: estimate assumptions, programme logic, risk register, scenario comparison and board narrative. That is valuable before consultants spend weeks creating a full baseline.

Why this matters commercially

Capital projects suffer from optimism bias, late risk visibility and fragmented reporting. AI project controls improves the speed of strategic analysis, not by removing experts but by giving them better first-pass structure.
