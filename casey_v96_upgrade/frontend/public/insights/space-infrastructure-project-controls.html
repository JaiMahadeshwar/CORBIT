Why space needs project controls

As space activity moves from missions to infrastructure, the delivery challenge begins to resemble megaprojects on Earth: capital allocation, long-lead procurement, logistics constraints, technical interfaces, stakeholder governance and delivery risk.

What makes space different

Earth megaprojectsSpace infrastructure
Site logisticsLaunch logistics and payload mass constraints
Weather and ground conditionsOrbital environment and radiation exposure
Procurement lead timesSpecialist manufacturing and qualification cycles
Commissioning riskRemote operations and limited maintainability

The first controls layer

Space projects need early project-control artefacts: estimate basis, schedule logic, risk register, assumptions register, scenario model, procurement exposure and governance summary. CONTROLORBIT is designed around this intelligence layer.

A credible market wedge

The commercial opportunity is not to claim certainty in an uncertain domain. It is to make uncertainty visible, structured and decision-useful before capital is committed.
