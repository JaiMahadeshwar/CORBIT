# v25 Earth + Space Full Demo Script

## Opening line
“Give CASEY any project on Earth or in Space. It produces the first boardroom controls pack before the consultancy team has booked the workshop.”

## Earth example
Use: `$5bn AI data centre in Saudi Arabia accelerated, class 3 level 4, 500MW, high grid and cooling risk`

Show:
- Executive confidence explanation
- Faster / cheaper / lower-risk scenarios
- Cost estimate and basis of impact
- Level 4 schedule and Level 1-5 export coverage
- Risk register mapped to WBS/CBS
- QCRA/QSRA P-curves and tornado drivers
- Peer operators / similar projects
- Download full pack

## Space example
Use: `Moon hospital for 1,000 people with closed-loop life support and radiation shielding`

Show:
- Space-specific cost lines: launch, ECLSS, thermal, shielding, robotics, operations
- Activity-risk mapping: launch integration, habitat assembly, ECLSS commissioning
- QCRA/QSRA Monte Carlo curves
- Space peer intelligence and analogue benchmarks
- Board report / PPTX / XER / Excel output pack

## Closing line
“This does not replace expert judgement. It replaces the blank page, compresses weeks of early analysis into minutes, and makes every assumption visible.”
