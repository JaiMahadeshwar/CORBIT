# CASEY V147 — Full Demo Script (Earth + Space)

## Opening line
"Give CASEY any project on Earth or in Space. It produces the first boardroom controls pack before the advisory team has booked the kick-off workshop."

---

## Earth demo — step by step

**Use this brief:**
`$5bn AI data centre in Saudi Arabia accelerated, class 3 level 4, 500MW, high grid and cooling risk`

### 1. Executive dashboard (30 sec)
Show P50 cost, P10/P90 range, schedule, risk posture and confidence score.
Say: "This is the first board-defensible view. One brief, no workshop, no data room."

### 2. Scenarios (45 sec)
Click through **Base → Faster → Cheaper → Lower Risk → Premium**.
Say: "Every scenario shows what changed and why — not just a different number."

### 3. Cost estimate (30 sec)
Show Class 3 cost lines with P10/P50/P90 by CBS account, location factors and basis of estimate.
Say: "AACE Class 1 through 5 all generated from the same model."

### 4. Schedule (30 sec)
Show Level 4 schedule. Mention XER export for Primavera P6.
Say: "Level 1 through 5 coverage, P6-compatible XER, generated live."

### 5. Risk map (30 sec)
Show every risk mapped to its WBS activity (QSRA driver) and CBS cost account (QCRA driver).
Say: "Every risk is traceable. Not a list — a map."

### 6. Monte Carlo — QCRA / QSRA (45 sec)
Show P-curves for cost and schedule. Show tornado drivers.
Say: "These are not sensitivity tables. These are full Monte Carlo runs with the risk register wired in."

### 7. Peers (30 sec)
Show operator-grade benchmark comparisons for similar projects.
Say: "This is owner intelligence, not consultancy benchmarking. Clients use this to challenge their own advisors."

### 8. Advisor — THE NEW STEP (60 sec)
Open the **Advisor / Chat** tab with the live model loaded.

Ask these three questions in sequence — show the answers live:

- **"What will the board attack?"**
  → CASEY returns the five hardest board questions specific to this project type.

- **"Where do you disagree with the reported position?"**
  → CASEY explains what the numbers say versus what a status report would say.

- **"If this programme fails, what will the headline say?"**
  → CASEY gives the most likely public failure narrative and the internal reason behind it.

Say: "This is not a chatbot. It is governance intelligence grounded in the model you just generated. The board questions it gives you are the ones your advisors spend days preparing."

### 9. Exports (30 sec)
Open the output centre. Show all formats: Excel, risk register, XER, Word board report, PDF intelligence pack, PPTX board deck, JSON audit model, full ZIP.
Say: "Every format, generated live, from the same model."

## Space demo — step by step

**Use this brief:**
`Moon hospital for 1,000 people with closed-loop life support and radiation shielding`

### 1. Show the model flips to Space mode automatically
Cost lines shift: launch, ECLSS, habitat, thermal shielding, robotics, autonomous construction, operations.
Say: "Same engine, different world. It knows the difference between a data centre and a lunar hospital."

### 2. Risk map — space-specific risks
Show launch integration, ECLSS reliability, mass growth, radiation exposure, habitat assembly.
Say: "These are not Earth construction risks with 'space' written on them. These are mission-specific risk drivers."

### 3. Monte Carlo
Show the wider P10/P90 range and higher P80 schedule exposure.
Say: "Lower confidence is correct for space. CASEY does not fake certainty it has not earned."

### 4. Peer intelligence
Show space programme analogues and benchmarks.
Say: "Lunar Base Alpha, Artemis infrastructure, orbital data centres — CASEY has seen these."

### 5. Board report / exports
Download the full output pack.
Say: "Board report, PPTX deck, XER, risk register, QCRA/QSRA workbook — for a Moon hospital. In under 3 minutes."

## Demo gate notes (for the operator)
- Public visitors: **one free run per IP address** — enforced in production.
- Your admin emails (set in `CASEY_ADMIN_EMAILS`) bypass the gate entirely — unlimited runs.
- On your local machine, set `CASEY_LOCAL_DEMO=1` in backend `.env` to disable the gate for testing.
- To reset a specific test email lock: `POST /elite-demo/reset-test-email` from localhost.
